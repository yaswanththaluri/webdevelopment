Our History



A J Computers provide a computer and laptop repair service to individuals and businesses throughout the Winchester and Hempshire area. Helping customers since 2005, the business is based in central Winchester. The company is run by Adam Bates, a Microsoft and Comptia certified IT technician, who is proud to offer a local, friendly and professional IT support service. Call or contact Adam today and receive a prompt resolution to computer, laptop and network troubles.

A J Computers also specialise in small business I.T support and have a dynamic framework in place to provide cost effective and comprehensive I.T support that will underpin any SME.
